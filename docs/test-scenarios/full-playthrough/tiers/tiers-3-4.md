# Tiers 3–4 — steel, coal power, and Phase 2

Set tier to 4 on entry; unlock alts ≤ T4. This group industrializes the
run: coal power replaces biomass babysitting, steel opens, water becomes a
real input, and Mk3 belts + Miner Mk.2 roughly double throughput ceilings.

## Unlocks (per library data)

- **Buildings:** Foundry (T3), Water Extractor (T3), Miner Mk.2 (T4)
- **Belts/pipes:** Mk3 belts — 270/min (T4); Mk1 pipes — 300 m³/min (T3)
- **Recipes:** T3 — Steel Ingot, Steel Beam, Steel Pipe, Versatile
  Framework, Power Shards, black powder family; T4 — Encased Industrial
  Beam, Stator, Motor, Automated Wiring
- **Power:** coal generators (T3) — the first water consumers

## Build objectives

1. **Steel Mill** (new, at a coal + iron pairing — e.g. the northern coast
   coal field) — Steel Ingot 45/min feeding Steel Beam 10/min, Steel Pipe
   15/min, Encased Industrial Beam 4/min (concrete via link from Copper
   Works or a local limestone claim — pick by distance and show it).
2. **Coal Power Station** — coal generators sized to carry the whole grid
   at this group's end (state total MW in the artifact). Water extractor
   groups on an approved water body next to the plant; pipes from water to
   generators must respect the 300 m³/min pipe cap per segment.
3. **Motor Assembly** (extend Iron Works or new) — Stator 5/min, Motor
   2.5/min, Automated Wiring 2.5/min.
4. **Elevator Yard** — add Versatile Framework 5/min. **Phase 2 needs
   Smart Plating + Versatile Framework + Automated Wiring** — wire the
   imports and state the delivery rates.
5. **Miner upgrades** — re-claim the busiest nodes onto Mk.2 where it
   relieves a belt or supply bottleneck; record which claims changed.

## Logistics notes

- Fluids exist now (water). Pipe segments cap at 300 m³/min; a 600 m³/min
  generator bank needs two headers. Show pipe runs in the power station
  layout.
- Mk3 belts (270/min) make single-belt manifolds practical for most lines;
  note where upgrades removed Tier-0/2 parallel-belt workarounds.
